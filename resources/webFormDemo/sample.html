Moose Rights

One of Canada's most divisive issues leading into the 2018 election cycle is that of Moose Rights. While beavers have had the privilege of voting since being declared our national animal in 1975, moose-kind has been left behind. Some argue that accommodating such large animals in a voting booth would be logistically challenging, others still have trouble accepting moose as more than mere pets (a practice now largely frowned upon), while many are outraged that moose are still struggling for such a basic mammal right.
